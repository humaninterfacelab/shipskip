Build a complete SaaS landing page for a product called "Flowmatic" — an AI-powered workflow automation tool that helps small teams eliminate repetitive tasks and ship faster.

**Brand personality:** Precise, confident, slightly technical — like a well-engineered tool, not a toy. Think clean editorial meets industrial utility.

**Aesthetic direction:** Dark theme. Near-black background (#0c0c0e). Sharp typographic hierarchy using a geometric sans-serif display font paired with a neutral mono or condensed body font. Accent color: electric amber (#f5a623). Subtle grain texture overlay on the hero. Grid-line decorative elements. No soft gradients — sharp, intentional.

**Key copy to use:**
- Hero headline: "Automate the work. Keep the thinking."
- Hero subheadline: "Flowmatic connects your tools, eliminates busywork, and keeps your team in flow — without writing a single line of code."
- Primary CTA: "Start for free" (links to #pricing)
- Secondary CTA: "See it in action" (links to #how-it-works)

**Features to highlight:**
1. Visual Workflow Builder — drag-and-drop automation in minutes
2. 200+ Integrations — Slack, Notion, GitHub, Linear, and more
3. Smart Triggers — event-based, schedule-based, or AI-inferred
4. Team Permissions — role-based access with audit logs
5. Real-time Monitoring — live run logs and failure alerts
6. SOC 2 Compliant — enterprise-grade security out of the box

**Pricing tiers:**
- Starter: Free — 3 workflows, 500 runs/mo
- Pro: $29/mo — unlimited workflows, 50k runs/mo (mark as "Most popular")
- Enterprise: Custom — SSO, SLA, dedicated support

**Testimonials:** Write 3 realistic, specific testimonials from fictional personas (name, role, company).

**FAQ:** Write 5 realistic questions a prospect would ask before signing up.
